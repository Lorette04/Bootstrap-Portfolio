About Me

Am I a SuperHero?
Am I hiding behind this picture?
I guess not. Let’s find out…

Here, I am. A multitasking French lady, expert in marketing – from marketing and economic research to product life cycle management – and in management – projects, products, people, lives (?)… I lived in different countries, with different people, I have to adapt to any situation. That gives you at some point a positive attitude.
Willing to help others and share; My motto is “There may be problems, but definitively always solutions”. With that being said, it is natural for me to have ideas any time, sometimes great, sometimes not that great. And I really think there is a hero that sleeps inside us; we just have to work hard to find out where he is.

So, Yes, I Am a SuperHero, or may I be more specific?, SuperHeroine (yes, let’s use the old fashioned form!!)
